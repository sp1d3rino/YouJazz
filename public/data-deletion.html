Data Deletion Instructions - YouJazz

Last updated: December 13, 2025

This page explains how you can request the deletion of your personal data collected by YouJazz (https://www.youjazz.org).

We respect your right to have your personal data removed. You can request deletion at any time.

How to Request Data Deletion

Option 1: Delete via Logout (Recommended)

Since your YouJazz account is linked exclusively to your Google or Facebook account:

  • Simply log out of YouJazz (using the "Logout" option in the menu).
  • We do not store login credentials â€” your account exists only while you are logged in with Google or Facebook.
  • If you do not log in again, your session will expire and no further personal data will be associated with new activity.

Option 2: Full Data Deletion Request (Permanent Removal)

If you want us to permanently delete all personal data associated with your account (including your profile information, saved songs, and any other stored data):

  1. Send an email to the address below with the subject: "Data Deletion Request - YouJazz"
  2. Include in the email:
    • Your display name on YouJazz
    • The email address used for login (Google or Facebook email)
    • Optional: Any song titles or details to help us identify your account
  3. We will verify the request and permanently delete your data from our database within 30 days.
  4. You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete.
Note: Deleting your data is irreversible. You will lose access to all saved songs and preferences. Public songs created by you (if any) may be anonymized or removed at our discretion.

Data Stored by Third Parties

YouJazz uses Google and Facebook only for authentication. We do not store your social media passwords. If you wish to revoke YouJazz access:

  • For Google: Go to https://myaccount.google.com/permissions and remove "YouJazz".
  • For Facebook: Go to Facebook Settings > Apps and Websites and remove "YouJazz".

Contact for Deletion Requests

Please send all data deletion requests to:

Email: [your contact email, e.g. privacy@youjazz.org or info@youjazz.org]

We will respond within 7 business days.

Thank you for using YouJazz.
